# Code of Conduct

ResearchForge contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, constructive, and focused on building reproducible research software.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, disciplines, and experience levels.
- Prefer evidence, tests, and clear reasoning over personal criticism.
- Give and receive technical feedback constructively.
- Keep discussions focused on project goals, safety, reproducibility, and user value.
- Respect privacy, copyright, research ethics, and data-sensitivity boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Deliberately submitting secrets, private data, or unauthorized copyrighted material.
- Repeated disruption of technical discussions or review processes.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, restrict participation, or take other reasonable moderation actions to protect the project and its contributors.

If a concern arises, contact the maintainers through the repository owner or project communication channel.
