# Screening scenarios

Minimum scenarios to encode as tests:

- include at title/abstract stage advances to full-text stage;
- exclude requires a configured reason;
- uncertain decisions appear in an uncertainty queue;
- two reviewers can disagree and create a conflict;
- PRISMA counts distinguish identified, deduplicated, screened, excluded, assessed full text, and included records;
- CSV export/import round-trips decisions without losing provenance fields.
